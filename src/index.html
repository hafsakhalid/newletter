What is Proof Zero

Proof Zero solves core problems in enterprise data management that other solutions typically push back onto customers. Most data initiatives fail because of garbage-in-garbage-out problems. Teams of genius data scientists working with top-of-the-line infrastructure to make world-class models can’t fight uphill against bad data that they can’t safely access.
We started Proof Zero because we saw that companies were solving the same data problems over and over again by throwing developers and data scientists at them. We built a better way so that all of those people can work on higher-value, more interesting problems
