What is Proof Zero

With Proof Zero, technical teams are freed from endless integration and data management projects. Our patent-pending data fidelity solution  connects and corrects customer data, improving data quality and integrity.
Proof Zero subscribers unlock hidden customer journeys and discover new insights. 
We want to help teams of genius data scientists by giving them the tools and ownership to fight the ongoing battle against bad data. We started Proof Zero because we saw that companies were attempting to solve the same data problems over and over again by throwing developers and data scientists at them without much progress.
We built a better way so that all of those people can work on higher-value, more interesting problems.
